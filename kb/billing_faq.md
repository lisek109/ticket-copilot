## Common issues
- Missing invoice
- Incorrect amount
- Duplicate charge
- Payment not reflected

## First checks
1. Verify customer/company name and billing period
2. Check invoice status in billing system
3. Confirm payment method and date

## Typical resolutions
- **Missing invoice:** resend PDF, confirm email address
- **Incorrect amount:** compare against contract / price list
- **Duplicate charge:** create refund request, mark ticket as billing-incident
- **Payment not reflected:** check bank settlement delay (up to 2 business days)

## Escalation
Escalate to Finance when:
- amount discrepancy cannot be explained
- customer disputes charge formally
- legal/compliance language is used
